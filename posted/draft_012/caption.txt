Florida Housing Market — What Buyers Need to Know This Summer ☀️🏡

A few things worth knowing if you're house hunting in Florida right now:

📦 Inventory is improving. There are more homes on the market than this time last year, which means buyers have slightly more options and a bit less pressure. That said, well-priced homes in good condition are still moving quickly.

📊 Rates have been range-bound over recent weeks, though day-to-day movement continues based on economic data releases. No one can predict where rates will be in 60 days — what matters is where they land on the day YOU lock.

🏆 VA buyers have a built-in edge. The VA loan's no-PMI benefit means veterans come out ahead on monthly payment compared to a conventional buyer at the same rate — and that gap is meaningful right now.

📍 Tampa Bay spotlight: demand in the Tampa metro remains solid. If you're thinking about buying in Hillsborough, Pinellas, or Pasco counties, starting the pre-approval process now means you'll be ready to move when the right home comes up.
