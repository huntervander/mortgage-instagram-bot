FHA, VA, or USDA — which loan program is actually right for you? Here's a quick breakdown. 🏡📋

🎖️ VA Loan — Best if you qualify
✅ $0 down payment
✅ No PMI ever
✅ Competitive rates
⚠️ Requires military service eligibility
→ If you're a veteran or active duty, start here. It almost always wins.

🏠 FHA Loan — Best for flexible credit
✅ 3.5% down minimum (580+ credit score)
✅ Lower credit thresholds than conventional
✅ Available to any buyer
⚠️ Mortgage insurance (MIP) for the life of the loan in most cases
→ Great for buyers with limited credit history or smaller down payments

🌾 USDA Loan — Best for eligible rural/suburban areas
✅ $0 down payment
✅ No PMI (small guarantee fee instead)
✅ Available to any buyer in eligible areas
⚠️ Property must be in a USDA-eligible location; income limits apply
→ Underused and underrated — many Florida areas qualify

Conventional loans (not above) tend to win when you have strong credit AND a solid down payment — lower long-term cost without MIP.
