Did you know Florida has programs that can help cover your down payment? 🏠

If saving up for a down payment is the biggest thing standing between you and homeownership, you may have more options than you think — especially right here in Florida.

Here are three programs worth knowing:

🏡 Florida Hometown Heroes — Built for community workforce members (teachers, nurses, first responders, and more). Offers down payment and closing cost assistance up to 5% of the first mortgage loan amount.

🏡 Florida Housing First Mortgage Programs — 30-year, fixed-rate loans paired with down payment assistance for eligible buyers. Income and purchase price limits apply by county.

🏡 FHA Loans — Require as little as 3.5% down for buyers with a 580+ credit score. One of the most popular options for first-time buyers who haven't had years to build up savings.

The right program for you depends on your income, credit profile, and the home you're buying. That's exactly why speaking with a licensed loan officer before you start your home search can save you thousands of dollars.
