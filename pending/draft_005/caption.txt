Found a house you love but haven't talked to a lender yet? Here's why that's risky. 🏡🔑

Getting pre-approved BEFORE you start house hunting isn't just a formality — it's one of the smartest moves a buyer can make:

✅ You'll know your real budget, so you don't waste time on homes outside your range
✅ Sellers take your offer more seriously when it's backed by a pre-approval letter
✅ It can speed up your closing timeline once you find "the one"
✅ It only takes a few minutes of paperwork to get started

Pre-approval isn't a commitment — it's information. And it puts you in a much stronger position the moment you're ready to make an offer.
