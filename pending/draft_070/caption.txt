Did you know your credit score can make or break your mortgage rate? 🏠📊

Here are 3 moves to strengthen your score before buying in Florida:

✅ Pay down revolving balances — aim to keep credit utilization under 30%. It's one of the fastest ways to move the needle.
✅ Avoid opening new credit accounts — new inquiries can temporarily dip your score right when you need it most.
✅ Pull your free credit report — errors are more common than you'd think. Dispute anything inaccurate at AnnualCreditReport.com before you apply.

Even a 20-40 point improvement could help you qualify for a better loan program and shave thousands off the life of your loan. Small moves, big impact. 💡
