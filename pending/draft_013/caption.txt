Not ready to commit to a full pre-approval yet? A pre-qualification is the perfect no-pressure first step. 👋🏡

Here's the difference:

📋 Pre-Qualification — a quick look at your finances based on info you provide. No hard credit pull in most cases. Gives you a general idea of what you might qualify for.

📝 Pre-Approval — a more formal review with documentation (pay stubs, bank statements, credit pull). Gives you a real number and a letter you can use when making offers.

Why start with a pre-qualification?
✅ Takes just a few minutes
✅ Helps you understand the ballpark before you start browsing homes
✅ No commitment — it's just information
✅ Identifies any issues early so you have time to address them

Most buyers who take a few minutes for a quick pre-qual are surprised by what they actually qualify for — in a good way.
