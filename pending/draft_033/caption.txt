Do you know what the VA loan benefit actually covers? 🇺🇸

If you served in the military — active duty, National Guard, or Reserves — you may be entitled to one of the most powerful home financing tools available. Here's what the VA loan really offers:

⭐ No down payment required — eligible buyers can purchase a home with $0 down
⭐ No private mortgage insurance (PMI) — this alone can save hundreds per month compared to conventional loans under 20% down
⭐ Competitive interest rates — VA loans often carry lower rates than comparable conventional options
⭐ No prepayment penalty — pay it off early without fees
⭐ A lifetime benefit — you can use it more than once

One important note: VA loans include a funding fee, which can typically be rolled into the loan. Veterans with a qualifying service-connected disability rating may be exempt from it entirely.

Not sure if you qualify? Eligibility is based on your length and type of service, discharge status, and other criteria. We're happy to walk you through it — no pressure, just honest guidance.
