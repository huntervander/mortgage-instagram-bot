What credit score do you actually need to buy a home in Florida? 🏡

Most buyers assume they need perfect credit — but that's a myth. Here's a quick breakdown by loan type:

🔵 Conventional Loan — Typically 620+ (lower score may mean higher rate or PMI)
🟢 FHA Loan — As low as 580 with 3.5% down; 500-579 may qualify with 10% down
⭐ VA Loan — No official VA minimum, but most lenders look for 580-620
🌾 USDA Loan — Usually 640+ for streamlined approval

Even if your score isn't quite where you want it, small steps can make a big difference in just a few months:
• Pay down credit card balances (keep utilization under 30%)
• Make all payments on time — even one missed payment stings
• Don't open new accounts right before applying

Every point counts, and a higher score can mean a lower rate over the life of your loan.
