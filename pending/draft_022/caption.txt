Florida has more first-time homebuyer programs than most people know about — and you don't have to figure them out alone. 🏡🌴

Here are some programs worth knowing:

🦸 Florida Hometown Heroes Program
Available to Florida's workforce — teachers, nurses, first responders, veterans, and others. Offers down payment and closing cost assistance plus below-market rate options. Income and purchase price limits apply.

🏠 Florida Housing Finance Corporation Programs
Statewide below-market mortgage rates paired with down payment assistance for income-eligible buyers.

💰 Florida Assist (Deferred Second Mortgage)
No monthly payments on this second mortgage — it's repaid when you sell, refinance, or pay off your first mortgage. Helps cover down payment and closing costs.

🎖️ Veterans buying for the first time: VA wins almost every time
No down payment, no PMI, competitive rates. Stack that with seller concessions and you can often close with very little out of pocket — even as a first-time buyer.

Programs have income limits, purchase price caps, and eligibility requirements that vary and can change. Talk to us to see exactly what applies to your situation.
