FHA or Conventional — which home loan is right for you? 🏡

If you're exploring your mortgage options, this is one of the most common questions we hear from first-time buyers. Here's a quick side-by-side:

🔵 FHA Loans:
• Minimum 3.5% down payment (with a 580+ credit score)
• More flexible credit qualifications
• Requires mortgage insurance premium (MIP) — typically for the life of the loan

🟡 Conventional Loans:
• As little as 3% down for qualifying buyers
• Private mortgage insurance (PMI) can be dropped once you reach 20% equity
• Generally requires a 620+ credit score

The "best" loan depends on YOUR credit profile, down payment savings, and long-term plans. There's no one-size-fits-all answer — that's exactly why we love walking through the numbers with our clients one-on-one.
