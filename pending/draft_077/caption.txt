Did you know you could buy a home with as little as 3.5% down? 🏡

FHA loans are one of the most popular options for Florida first-time homebuyers — and it's easy to see why.

Here's what makes them stand out:
• Low down payment — just 3.5% with a credit score of 580 or higher
• Flexible credit requirements — scores as low as 500 may still qualify (with 10% down)
• Higher debt-to-income allowances — you may still qualify even if you carry existing debt
• Can be combined with Florida's Hometown Heroes and other down payment assistance programs

One thing to understand: FHA loans require Mortgage Insurance Premiums (MIP) — both an upfront fee and a monthly charge. That's why it's worth comparing FHA vs. Conventional side by side to find what works best for your situation.
