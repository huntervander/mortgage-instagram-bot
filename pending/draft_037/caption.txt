Did you know your credit score could save — or cost — you thousands on your mortgage? 💡

Most buyers focus on the down payment, but your credit score is just as important. Lenders use it not just to approve your loan, but to price your interest rate. Even a small bump in your score can mean a lower monthly payment and real money back in your pocket over time.

Here are 4 simple tips to improve your score before you apply:

✅ Pay down credit card balances below 30% of your limit
✅ Avoid opening new credit accounts in the months before applying
✅ Set up autopay — one missed payment can drop your score fast
✅ Pull your free credit report and dispute any errors (they happen more than you think!)

The good news: you don't need a perfect score to buy a home in Florida. FHA loans may be available starting at 580, and conventional options can start at 620+. Every situation is different — let's look at yours together.
