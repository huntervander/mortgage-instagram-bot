Did you know most veterans can buy a home with $0 down? 🏡

The VA loan is one of the most powerful home financing benefits available to eligible service members, veterans, and surviving spouses — yet so many people still don't know how to use it.

Here's what makes the VA loan stand out:
✅ No down payment required (in most cases)
✅ No private mortgage insurance (PMI) — ever
✅ Competitive interest rates
✅ No prepayment penalties
✅ Reusable — you can use it more than once in your lifetime

Wondering if you qualify? Generally, active-duty service members, veterans, and select surviving spouses are eligible based on length and type of service.
