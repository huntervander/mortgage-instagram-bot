Your credit score could save you thousands on your mortgage. 💳

Before you apply for a home loan, a few small moves can make a big difference in the rate you're offered:

✅ Pay down credit card balances — lower utilization helps your score
🔍 Check your credit report for errors and dispute anything incorrect
🚫 Avoid opening new credit lines or making large purchases before applying
🕒 Keep older accounts open — length of credit history matters

Even a modest score increase can mean a better rate and lower monthly payment.
