Your credit score is one of the biggest factors lenders look at — do you know where yours stands? 📊

Here's a quick breakdown of common score minimums by loan type:
• Conventional loan: typically 620+
• FHA loan: as low as 580 (with 3.5% down)
• VA loan: no set minimum, but most lenders look for 580–620+

A higher credit score doesn't just help you qualify — it can unlock a significantly lower interest rate, potentially saving you thousands over the life of your loan.

Simple ways to strengthen your credit before you apply:
✅ Pay every bill on time — payment history is 35% of your score
✅ Keep credit card balances below 30% of your limit
✅ Avoid opening new credit accounts right before applying
✅ Pull your free credit report and check for errors (AnnualCreditReport.com)
