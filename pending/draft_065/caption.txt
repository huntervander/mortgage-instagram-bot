FHA vs. Conventional — which loan fits your situation? 🏡

If you're buying a home in Florida, you've probably heard both terms. Here's a quick breakdown to help you decide:

**FHA Loan:**
• Down payment as low as 3.5% (with 580+ credit score)
• Backed by the Federal Housing Administration
• More flexible qualification requirements — great for first-time buyers
• Requires mortgage insurance (MIP) — typically for the life of the loan

**Conventional Loan:**
• Down payments starting at 3% with a strong credit profile
• Follows Fannie Mae / Freddie Mac guidelines (not government-backed)
• Requires private mortgage insurance (PMI) if less than 20% down — but PMI can be removed once you reach 20% equity
• Generally requires a higher credit score (620+) to qualify

💡 There's no one-size-fits-all answer. The right loan depends on your credit profile, savings, and long-term goals.
