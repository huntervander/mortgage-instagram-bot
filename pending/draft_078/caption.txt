Did you know your credit score can save — or cost — you thousands over the life of your mortgage?

Here's how credit score tiers typically affect your home loan options:

🏆 740+ — Best rates available and access to the widest range of programs
✅ 680–739 — Strong options, including most conventional and FHA loans
⚠️ 620–679 — You can still qualify! FHA and VA programs are designed to help here
📋 Below 620 — Fewer options, but paths forward do exist — reach out and let's explore together

The good news? Your credit score isn't permanent. Paying bills on time, keeping credit card balances below 30% of your limit, and avoiding new credit applications can move the needle significantly in just a few months.

If you're not sure where you stand, pull a free copy of your credit report at AnnualCreditReport.com — no credit card required.
