VA loan myths are keeping veterans from using one of the best benefits they've earned. Let's set the record straight. 🎖️

❌ MYTH: VA loans take forever to close
✅ FACT: VA loans close in a similar timeframe to conventional loans when you work with an experienced lender

❌ MYTH: Sellers won't accept VA offers
✅ FACT: This perception is outdated. With a strong pre-approval and proper offer structure, VA offers are competitive

❌ MYTH: You can only use your VA benefit once
✅ FACT: You can use it multiple times. Pay off or sell a VA-financed home, restore your entitlement, and use it again

❌ MYTH: VA loans are only for combat veterans
✅ FACT: Eligibility is based on service length and type — not combat. Many non-combat veterans fully qualify

❌ MYTH: VA loans have lots of extra fees
✅ FACT: No PMI ever. The VA funding fee (a one-time cost) is often less than years of PMI payments — and veterans with a service-connected disability rating may be exempt entirely

Don't let myths keep you from a benefit you earned.
