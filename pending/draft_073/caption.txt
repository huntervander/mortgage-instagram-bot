Think you need perfect credit or a huge down payment to buy your first home in Florida? 🏠 An FHA loan might be the path forward.

FHA loans are government-backed mortgages designed to make homeownership more accessible — especially for first-time buyers or those still building their credit.

Here's what makes them stand out:
✅ As little as 3.5% down with a 580+ credit score
✅ More flexible credit requirements than conventional loans
✅ Sellers can contribute up to 6% toward closing costs
✅ Gift funds are allowed toward your down payment

Credit score between 500–579? You may still qualify with 10% down. In Florida's competitive real estate market, understanding all your loan options is a huge advantage — and FHA is one of the most popular choices for good reason.
