FHA vs. Conventional — which loan is right for you? 🏠

If you're buying your first (or next!) home in Florida, you've probably come across both terms. Here's a quick side-by-side:

📋 FHA Loan:
• Min. 3.5% down with a 580+ credit score
• More flexible credit requirements
• Requires Mortgage Insurance Premium (MIP) — often for the life of the loan
• A great option if you're still building credit history

📋 Conventional Loan:
• As little as 3% down for qualifying buyers
• Typically requires a 620+ credit score
• PMI can be removed once you reach 20% equity
• May offer better rates if you have strong credit

There's no one-size-fits-all answer — the right loan depends on your credit score, savings, and long-term goals. That's why it helps to talk it through with a licensed mortgage professional.
