📊 What's happening with mortgage rates and the Florida housing market right now?

As of late June 2026, rates remain elevated compared to the historic lows of a few years ago — but here's what that actually means for buyers:

📌 Buying in a higher-rate environment isn't a mistake — it's a strategy. You can always refinance when rates come down, but you can't go back and buy a home at 2021 prices.

📌 Florida demand is holding steady. Population growth and limited inventory continue to support home values across much of the state, especially in Tampa Bay, Orlando, and South Florida.

📌 Waiting for the "perfect" rate can cost you more than you think. Each month of delay means more rent paid and potential appreciation you miss out on.

📌 Your purchasing power may be higher than you expect. Down payment assistance programs and VA loan benefits can offset rate pressure significantly.
