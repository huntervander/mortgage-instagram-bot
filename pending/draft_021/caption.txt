When did you last actually look at whether your mortgage still makes sense? A refinance could be worth more than you think — or less. Here's how to know. 🔄🏡

Common reasons to refinance:
📉 Your rate is meaningfully higher than current market rates
💸 You need to access home equity (cash-out refinance)
📅 You want to shorten your loan term (e.g., 30 → 15 years)
📋 You want to remove PMI from a conventional loan now that you have 20%+ equity

The breakeven question:
Refinancing has upfront costs. The key question: how many months until your monthly savings cover those costs? If you plan to stay in the home past that point, a refi likely makes sense.

🎖️ VA homeowners have an extra option: the VA IRRRL (Interest Rate Reduction Refinance Loan), also called the VA Streamline. Designed specifically to lower your VA loan rate with minimal documentation and no appraisal required in most cases.
