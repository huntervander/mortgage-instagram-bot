If you've served, your VA loan benefit is one of the most powerful homebuying tools available. Here's what it actually includes. 🎖️🏡

✅ No down payment required — buy a home with $0 down (no, really)
✅ No private mortgage insurance (PMI) — most conventional loans require this until you hit 20% equity; VA loans skip it entirely
✅ Competitive interest rates — VA loans typically come in lower than conventional rates
✅ No prepayment penalty — pay it off early without any fees
✅ Funding fee waivers — veterans with a service-connected disability rating may be exempt from the VA funding fee entirely

The VA loan isn't just for first-time buyers either — eligible veterans and active service members can use the benefit multiple times.
