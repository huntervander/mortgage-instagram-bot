Your credit score could be the difference between thousands of dollars saved — or thousands lost — on your mortgage. Here's what to know before you apply. 🏡

📊 Credit score ranges and what they mean for home loans:
• 740+ → Strongest rates, most loan programs available
• 700–739 → Very good — competitive rates on Conventional, FHA, VA
• 620–699 → Good — solid options still available
• 580–619 → FHA may still be an option (talk to us first)
• Below 580 → Let's build a game plan together

💡 3 quick ways to boost your score before buying:
1. Pay every bill on time — payment history makes up 35% of your score
2. Keep credit card balances below 30% of your limit
3. Don't open new credit accounts while house-shopping — it can temporarily ding your score

Even a 20–40 point improvement can move you into a better rate tier and lower your monthly payment noticeably.
