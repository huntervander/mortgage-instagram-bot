Still waiting to save up 20% before buying a home? You might be waiting longer than you need to. 💡🏡

Let's bust some down payment myths:

❌ MYTH: You need 20% down
✅ FACT: Conventional loans can go as low as 3%, FHA as low as 3.5%, and VA loans require $0 down for eligible veterans

❌ MYTH: Lower down payment always means a worse deal
✅ FACT: VA loans offer $0 down with no PMI — often the best overall deal regardless of down payment size

❌ MYTH: There's no help available
✅ FACT: Programs like Florida's Hometown Heroes offer down payment and closing cost assistance to eligible buyers

❌ MYTH: A small down payment means you can't afford the home
✅ FACT: Lenders look at your full financial picture — income, debt load, credit, and reserves — not just your down payment amount

The 20% rule made sense in a different era. Today there are more paths to homeownership than most buyers realize.
