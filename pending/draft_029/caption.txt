💰 How much do you ACTUALLY need to put down?

One of the biggest myths in homebuying is that you need 20% down — but the truth is, you have way more options than you think.

Here's a quick breakdown by loan type:

🏠 Conventional — as low as 3% down (first-time buyers) or 5% for repeat buyers
🏡 FHA — 3.5% down with a 580+ credit score; great for buyers still building their credit profile
⭐ VA — $0 down for eligible veterans and active-duty service members
🌾 USDA — $0 down in qualifying rural areas of Florida
🌴 Florida Hometown Heroes — down payment & closing cost assistance for Florida's workforce

Every buyer's situation is different. Your credit, income, and goals all play a role in which loan fits best.
