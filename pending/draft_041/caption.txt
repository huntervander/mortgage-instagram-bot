FHA or Conventional — which loan is right for you? 🏡

If you're buying a home in Florida, choosing the right loan type can make a big difference in your monthly payment and long-term costs. Here's a quick breakdown:

🔵 FHA Loan
• Down payment as low as 3.5% (580+ credit score)
• More flexible credit guidelines — great if you're still building your score
• Requires mortgage insurance premium (MIP) — in most cases for the life of the loan

🔷 Conventional Loan
• Down payment as low as 3% for qualifying buyers
• PMI (private mortgage insurance) drops off automatically once you reach 20% equity
• Stronger rates with a 740+ credit score; minimum is typically 620+

The "right" choice depends on your credit score, how much you've saved, and your long-term plan. Florida also has assistance programs — like the Florida Hometown Heroes program — that can be layered with both loan types to help with down payment costs.
