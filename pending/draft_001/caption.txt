Did you know Florida has a program just for everyday heroes? 🏡

The Florida Hometown Heroes Program offers down payment and closing cost assistance to teachers, nurses, first responders, veterans, and other eligible workforce buyers — making homeownership more accessible across the state.

Here's what it can include:
🏅 Down payment & closing cost assistance
📉 Below-market interest rate options
✅ Available for first-time and some repeat buyers (income limits apply)

If you serve your community, there may be a program designed to help you join one too.
