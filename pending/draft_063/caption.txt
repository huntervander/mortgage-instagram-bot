🏡 Florida mortgage rates this summer — what buyers & homeowners need to know.

Rates have continued to shift in 2026, and staying informed is one of the best tools you have whether you're buying your first home or thinking about refinancing.

Here's what to keep in mind right now:

• Fixed-rate mortgages offer payment stability — great if you're planning to stay long-term
• ARM loans may offer lower initial rates for buyers who expect to move or refinance within 5–7 years
• Refinancing may still make sense if your current rate is significantly above today's market
• Florida's housing inventory is improving in many markets, giving buyers more options

Every rate environment has opportunities. The key is knowing which loan product fits your specific goals.
