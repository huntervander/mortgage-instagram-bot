FHA loans are one of the most popular loan types in the country — but there are a lot of misconceptions about who they're for and how they work. Here's the quick breakdown. 📋🏡

What makes FHA loans unique:
📉 Down payment as low as 3.5% (with a 580+ credit score)
🔧 More flexible credit requirements than conventional loans
💼 Works for first-time buyers AND repeat buyers (FHA isn't just for first-timers)
⚠️ Comes with Mortgage Insurance Premium (MIP) — an upfront charge plus a monthly amount that stays for the life of the loan in most cases

When FHA might make sense:
✅ You have a lower credit score or limited credit history
✅ You don't have a large down payment saved
✅ You need a more flexible underwriting path

When it might NOT make sense:
❌ Strong credit + solid down payment? Conventional could be cheaper long-term (no lifetime MIP)
❌ Veteran or active duty? VA almost always wins on overall value
