FHA vs. Conventional — which loan is actually right for you? 🏡

Two of the most common home loan types in Florida, but they're not one-size-fits-all. Here's what you need to know:

📋 **FHA Loans**
→ Backed by the Federal Housing Administration
→ Down payment as low as 3.5% with a 580+ credit score
→ More flexible credit requirements — great for first-time buyers
→ Requires mortgage insurance for the life of the loan (in most cases)

🏠 **Conventional Loans**
→ Not government-backed — held to lender standards
→ Typically requires a 620+ credit score (better rates with 700+)
→ PMI (private mortgage insurance) can be removed once you hit 20% equity
→ Often the better long-term option if your credit profile is strong

**Quick rule of thumb:** Credit score below 620? FHA may be your best path. Above 680 with some savings? Conventional could save you more over time.
